Who can join the dojo

To apprentices (computer scientists): to reinforce a concept they have seen in class, or to give them the basics so that they will be more comfortable in professional school.

To programmers: to discover and learn about other people's approaches, to go on technology tourism, to get out of their comfort zone or finally to network.

When dojos take place?

Dojos are held on Mondays and Fridays:

Monday: between 11:00 and 12:00 for apprentices. Friday: between 13:00 and 14:00 for experienced students.
